EX2:
Pt+1 = r * Pt * (1 - Pt)
The population growth strongly depends on the value of the growth factor r. The population starts to become chaotic at approximately r = 3.57
Behavior of the system:
- For smaller values of r, the population usually converges to a fixed value or oscillates periodically.
- Near r ≈ 3.57, the system becomes highly sensitive to the initial conditions.
- For larger values of r, the population fluctuates irregularly, indicating chaotic behavior.
